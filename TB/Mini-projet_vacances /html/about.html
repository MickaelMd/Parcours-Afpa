About this guide

While this guide provides a solid introduction to speedrunning in Minecraft, particularly for the Random Seed 1.16+ category, it's important to note that speedrunning is a vast and evolving field.
Many advanced techniques and strategies exist, and the community continuously develops new methods and optimizations.
For those looking to delve deeper into the world of Minecraft speedrunning, exploring additional resources, participating in forums, and watching high-level runs can provide valuable insights and help you further refine your skills.
